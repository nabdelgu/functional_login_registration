How it Works?

  • Create a free account, this will give you access to a calender to check for availability
  • Use this to check for tutoring sessions.
  • At the time of the session use the interactive whiteboard as well as audio capabilities enabled during the tutoring session
  • These features will connect me (The tutor) and you (The Student) in real time
  • We will be able to write/draw back and forth on the whiteboard, type in the chat box, and verbally communicate through the audio feature
  • The audio feature is higly reccomended but not required. You will be able to communicate through your browser
